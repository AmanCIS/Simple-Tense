Meet Our Staff

Jacob H.

Having worked as Assistant Editor in the publication of over five books for the College Music Society, Jacob H. is in charge of ensuring your writing and editing needs are met. He holds degrees from studies in the United States, Spain, and France, and joins the SimpleTense team with the experience of having written and edited hundreds of papers in a variety of subjects for clients with custom writing needs. He enjoys performing music, scuba diving, and reading.

Loren W.

Loren is a writer, editor, and theatre director, who began working with clients from SimpleTense’s parent company in 2002. She edits several online spaces, including one in collaboration with Canada's National Arts Centre and another that won ‘Best Arts and Culture Blog’ and ‘Best Blog Post’ in The Canadian Blog Awards. Her writing has been published in Lets Go Travel Guides, The Globe and Mail, Canadian Theatre Review, NOW Magazine and Fuse Magazine. As a theatre director she has directed twelve productions, including two Dora- nominated performances and has trained and coached actors for over a decade. Loren teaches an annual class for directors at Canada’s National Theatre School and spent a season as an assistant director at The Shaw Festival. Most recently, Loren has been working as a curator with Harbourfront Centre and completed a national tour of a play she directed that was an adaptation of a Facebook note for the stage.

Ashleigh S.

Ashleigh is a graduate of Yale University (B.A) and has been with SimpleTense since 2001. She has helped hundreds of clients with applications for numerous undergraduate programs and all of the top-tier programs for medical, pharmacy, graduate, PhD and MBA study. Ashleigh provides meticulous and sensitive edits that help clients convey their unique message effectively and completely - she perfects a client's writing while also working with the client to address critical areas that need improvement, all while maintaining the client's unique voice. Along with SimpleTense Ashleigh's editing experience includes work with publishing houses (editing books of a scientific nature), literary journals, and advertising firms. When she's not editing, Ashleigh can usually be found in the kitchen cooking up something yummy.

David S.

David studied at Stanford University (B.A. in International Relations) and served as a visiting scholar at the University of Oxford. He has helped students and professionals as an editor for 7 years and started as a Stanford Peer Writing Tutor where he trained students to communicate with clear, concise, and compelling writing. He joined SimpleTense in 2009, and has since helped hundreds of college hopefuls, students, and professionals discover their unique storyline, showcase their strengths, and progress towards their goals. David listens carefully to clients’ needs and delivers detailed edits to ensure that academic and professional strengths are highlighted while respecting the client’s original voice. He equips clients to succeed with both writing and interviews and focuses on the connection between clients’ experiences, aspirations, and anticipated future impacts in their respective field. In the process, he has coached native English speaking and ESL clients with college, graduate, business, law, and medicine applications and helped them gain admission to top-tier schools in the U.S. and internationally. In addition to editing, David works full-time in corporate finance with a focus on investor relations and corporate communications. He is also invested in social entrepreneurship and served as Marketing Manager for two Bangkok-based enterprises and previously judged for the Global Social Ventures Competition in Asia. He enjoys identifying promising ideas, talented leaders, and connecting them with opportunities to have impact. David also enjoys running, cooking, eating, reading, travel, and geopolitics. Originally from the San Francisco Bay Area, David is based in Bangkok, Thailand.
